little robot is Stuart; a freelance web developer in St Ives, Cambridgeshire.

I specialise in working with small business to solve their problems with computers, helping people sell via the Internet, and developing websites of all shapes and sizes.

How I can help

  • I make you more efficient

    If you can think of a process for performing a task, I can get a computer to do it for you. From taking information from one place and converting it for use somewhere else to reporting on your data in real time, delegate the boring a repetitive tasks to a computer and focus on the important stuff.

  • I help you make more money

    I can create a website that will allow you to take payment by credit card, PayPal or any other payment method your clients want to use. Flexible but easy to use features mean the site will be completely maintainable by you, whatever your product.

  • I get you found on the Internet

    Kick-start your web presence or replace your poorly performing site with an eye-catching website that works on whatever device your target audience uses; be it a desktop, laptop, tablet or smartphone. You can even manage all your own content from just about any Internet connected device.

How it works

The process is designed to ensure you get exactly what you want and need, and can be customised to the individual project.

Plan

I spend time with you to find out what you want to achieve, work out the best way to achieve this and write it all up in an easy to understand proposal so you know exactly what to expect.

Build

Once the proposal is signed off, I start work on your project. I'll ask for regular feedback to make sure you're completely happy.

Acceptance

Once all the features are complete, you'll have a final chance to ensure everything is exactly how you want it to be.

Launch

Once complete, the project will be made available on reliable systems so that it can be accessed by you and your customers.
